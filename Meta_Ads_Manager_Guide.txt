================================================================================
META ADS MANAGER — CAMPAIGN CREATION WALKTHROUGH
================================================================================

When you click "Create" you see:

  • Create new campaign
  • New ad set or ad
  • Buying type: Auction
  • Campaign objective: choose one of the options below


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. CAMPAIGN OBJECTIVES (OVERVIEW)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  AWARENESS
  ---------
  Show your ads to people who are most likely to remember them.
  Good for: Reach | Brand awareness | Video views

  TRAFFIC
  -------
  Send people to a destination (website, app, Instagram profile, Facebook event).
  Good for: Link clicks | Landing page views | Instagram profile visits |
            Messenger, Instagram and WhatsApp | Calls

  ENGAGEMENT
  ----------
  Get more messages, purchases through messaging, video views, post engagement,
  Page likes or event responses.
  Good for: Messenger, Instagram and WhatsApp | Video views | Post engagement |
            Conversions | Calls

  LEADS
  -----
  Collect leads for your business or brand.
  Good for: Website and instant forms | Instant forms |
            Messenger, Instagram and WhatsApp | Conversions | Calls

  APP PROMOTION
  -------------
  Find new people to install your app and continue using it.
  Good for: App installs | App events

  SALES
  -----
  Find people likely to purchase your product or service.
  Good for: Conversions | Catalog sales |
            Messenger, Instagram and WhatsApp | Calls


================================================================================
2. AWARENESS — FLOW (STEP BY STEP)
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Campaign details
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Campaign name
  • Campaign details
  • Buying type: Auction
  • Campaign objective: Awareness

  Show more options:
  • Advantage+ campaign budget — Off
    Distribute your budget across ad sets to get more results. You can control
    spending for each ad set.
  • A/B test — Off
    Compare versions to see what works best; each shown to separate audience groups.
  • Special Ad Categories
    Declare if ads relate to financial products, employment, housing, social
    issues, elections or politics. Requirements differ by country.
  • Categories — Select the categories that best describe this campaign.
    Declare category if applicable.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 2: Placements
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Account controls — Excluded placements: None
  • Advantage+ placements
    Budget allocated by Meta across multiple placements based on likely performance.

  Hide options:
  • Brand safety and suitability
    Meta applies brand safety via Community Standards and Monetization Policies.
    Brand suitability can add filters/topic exclusions (may lower reach, increase cost).
  • Inventory filters
    Default filter applied. Excessively controversial/offensive content always excluded.
  • In-content ads — Expanded (ad set)
  • Audience Network ads — Expanded (ad set)
  • Publisher block lists — None selected
  • Content type exclusions — None selected

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 3: Ad setup
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Ad creative — Select and optimize ad text, media and enhancements.
  • Creative testing — Compare up to 5 creative versions.
  • Chat builder — Choose a template for the chat after people tap your ad.
    Suggested chat template:
    - Welcome message: "Hi! Please let us know how we can help you."
    - Add a call to action
    - Pre-filled message: "Hello! Can I get more info on this?"
  • Tracking — Conversion events (website events, app events, offline events).
  • URL parameters — Build a URL parameter.


================================================================================
3. TRAFFIC — FLOW (STEP BY STEP)
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Campaign details
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Buying type: Auction
  • Campaign objective: Traffic

  Show more options:
  • Budget
    - Budget strategy: Campaign budget (Advantage+ campaign budget) or Ad set budget.
    - Campaign budget: Automatically distribute budget to best opportunities.
    - Ad set budget: Set different bid strategies or budget schedules per ad set.
    - Share some of your budget with other ad sets — up to 20% when it may improve
      performance.
  • Campaign bid strategy — Highest volume
  • A/B test — Off
  • Special Ad Categories — Declare if applicable.
  • Categories — Select categories for this campaign.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 2: Advantage+ audience & placements
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Advantage+ audience
    Meta shows ads to people most likely to respond, using your suggestion and
    other audiences when it may improve performance.
    Option: Switch to original audience options.

  • Ad transparency
    Show who benefits from and pays for ads (may appear in Ad Library).
    Build trust by completing verification.

  • Placements
    Where your ad appears across Meta technologies.
    Excluded placements: None.
    Advantage+ placements — budget allocated by Meta for best performance.
    Show more options available.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 3: Ad setup
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Destination — Where to send people when they tap or click:
    - Call phone number (country code, valid number)
    - Enable callback requests — Off
    - Callback: spreadsheet or Messenger
    - Call on WhatsApp

  • Ad creative — Select and optimize ad text, media and enhancements.
    Note: "Please specify an image to run with this ad."

  • Creative testing — Compare up to 5 versions.
  • Tracking — Website events, app events, offline events.


================================================================================
4. ENGAGEMENT — FLOW (STEP BY STEP)
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 1: Campaign details
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Buying type: Auction
  • Campaign objective: Engagement

  Show more options:
  • Budget — Same structure as Traffic (campaign vs ad set budget, sharing, etc.).
  • Campaign bid strategy — Highest volume
  • A/B test — Off
  • Special Ad Categories — Declare if applicable.
  • Categories — Select categories for this campaign.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 2: Conversion, audience & placements
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Conversion
    - Conversion location: Calls
    - Performance goal: How you measure success.
      Example: Maximize number of calls — deliver ads to get the most possible calls.
    - Facebook Page — Choose the Page you want to promote (Select Page).
    - Value rules — Tell Meta how much more certain audiences, conversion
      locations and placements are worth; system optimizes for these.
    - Delivery type: Standard

  • Advantage+ audience — Same as Traffic (auto audience + option to switch to
    original audience options).
  • Ad transparency — Same as Traffic.
  • Placements — Same as Traffic (Advantage+ placements, excluded placements, etc.).
  • Show more options available.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 3: Ad setup
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Multi-advertiser ads
    Your ad can appear with others in the same ad unit; creative may be resized
    or cropped.

  • Ad creative — Select and optimize ad text, media and enhancements.
    Note: "A post is required. Select or create a post to publish."
    Option: Enter post ID.

  • Creative testing — Compare up to 5 versions.
  • Tracking — Website events, app events, offline events.
